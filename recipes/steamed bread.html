Fluffy Mini Steamed Bread

Once you try this mini steamed breads you won't stop eating them! All you need is an all-purpose flour, salt, sugar, olive oil and instant yeast. You can serve these with beef or chicken stew, or just with hot or cold beverages.It's perfect for cold weathers with soup, don't say i did'nt tell you!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups All-purpose flour/li>
  • salt
  • 1 tbs Sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Instant yeast
  • 1 cup Warm water
  • 2 tbs Olive oil

Steps to follow:

  1. Get a large bowl and pour your pinch of salt, 1 tbs sugar, instant yeast and flour but sift your flour first. Mix your dry ingredients using a whisk until well-combined.
  2. Add 2 tbs of olive oil in 1 cup of warm water then pour in the dry ingredients bowl.
  3. Mix well until well-combined then kneed for 10 minutes. Cover your bowl with dough using a plastic wrap and let it raise for 15 minutes.
  4. Kneed the door after it has risen then divide the dough into 10 equal parts. Roll each and every part into a small balls.
  5. Spray ramekins with a non-sticky spray then after 2 seconds then place your small dough balls.
  6. In a big pot with boiling water, place the ramekins then close the pot. Steam them for 30 minutes.
  7. Carefully remove the ramekins from the pot and let the cool for at least 6 minutes. Boom your mini steam breads are READY!
