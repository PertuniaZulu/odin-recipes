Mac and Cheese

This is a creamy and delicious mac and cheese that is very easy to make. All you need is milk,macaroni,any cheese of your choice, flour and butter to make the rich and creamy sauce, olive oil and spices to add flavour and aroma. The dish can be served with fried chicken breasts or grilled beef strips.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Cheese
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Olive oil
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Pour 1 cup of macaroni in boiling water with 1 tsp of olive oil and salt then boil until soft. Drain then put it aside for later.
  2. Melt butter in a pan then pour 2 cups of milk in a pan then add your spices then when it boils pour 3 tbs of flour, whisk until the lumps dissolves.
  3. Add grated cheese bring it to simmer then pour the boiled macaroni
  4. Mix well then pour in a bowl. Your dish is ready!
