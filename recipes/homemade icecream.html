Homemade Vanilla Ice cream in 20 Minutes!

Craving ice cream but you are lazy to go buy it? I got you. Follow this simple recipe, with just four ingredients! Total time:Prepare for 5 minutes and process or freeze for 15 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of heavy whipping cream
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 2 cups of cream
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Mix all the ingredients by whisking until sugar dissolves completely.
  2. Fill the container of tub (make sure that you cover the container) place in the freezer for 20 minutes.
  3. Serve after 20 minutes. You can add any berries that you love for decoration.

    4. Enjoy your desert!!!
